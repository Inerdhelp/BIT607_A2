FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What shampoo or conditioner do you use?

To make sure your pets get premium care, we forged a partnership with a Filipino‑made luxury pet shampoo and conditioner manufacturer. Our exclusive in‑house formulation is hypoallergenic, non‑toxic, sulfate‑free, paraben‑free, cruelty‑free, and organic.

How long does grooming take?

The duration of grooming depends on the size and breed of your pet as well as the services requested. Generally, it takes between 2 to 4 hours.

Can I walk-in? Do I need reservation?

You may walk-in, but reservations are highly encouraged.

Cancellations & no shows

If you need to cancel/ reschedule your appointment(s), we respectfully ask that you provide us with 24 hours notice prior to the booking.
Failure to do so will result in a charge of 50% of the service or loss of deposit.
No shows will result in a 100% charge of the booked service.
